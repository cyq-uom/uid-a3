Cinnamon huntsman

A medium sized species of huntsman found in eucalypt forests around Adelaide, SA. Officially called Isopedella leai, they are a light brown colour with banding on the legs and has a spotted abdomen.

Adults have a body length of 30mm, and a leg-span of up to 100mm while Juveniles have a body length of 8-12mm, and a leg-span 20mm
